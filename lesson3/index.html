Site Purpose

The purpose of forecastpin is to provide up-to-date weather forecast wherever users pin there location. The site will provide the current weather conditions such as current temperature, predicted high and low temperature, wind speed, precipitation, humidity, ten-day forecast, and predicted weather (i.e sunny, rain, snow, fog, overcast,etc.). The site will be clean and easy to use.
