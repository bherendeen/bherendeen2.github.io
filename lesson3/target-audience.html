Site Plan - Audience

Target Audience

The targeted audience for forecastpin are individuals that would like to know the current and forecasted weather in their area or the location they’ll be visiting, weather articles, 10-day forecast, and to be able to plan their outdoor activities accordingly.

  • Who: The site is for both males and females who have access to the internet.
  • Age: 14+
  • Income: All incomes

Weather affects the human race as a whole, therefore the site is for the use of the general public as a whole.
